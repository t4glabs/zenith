Shivpuri Youth Forum

The Shivpuri Youth Forum (SYF) is a youth-led collective aimed at empowering and engaging young people in meaningful activities. Established in 2014, it provides a platform for local youth from Shivpuri towards inspiration, creativity and innovation.

Due to the s and cities, The platform caters to the lack of safe physical spaces in small towns by providing a reading space, organising cultural events, workshops, and debates - fostering a space for growth, expression, and breaking stereotypes. SYF leads an environment campaign called "Shivpuri Jalashay Sanrakshan Abhiyaan," with an attempt to restore the rich biodiversity and natural ecosystem of Shivpuri through collective action. SYF also successfully runs the kalam library - a library and counselling center to learn and engage with the aid of materials, newspapers and digital access to resources. The forum's vision is to build local resources and spaces, enabling equal access to opportunities and nurturing young leaders within the community.

Anil Diwan Legal Resource Centre

Anil Divan Foundation has supported the setting up of a legal resource center for law students & young lawyers in Shivpuri. The Foundation has provided infrastructural support in setting up of the center by generously donating books & journals of hon’ble Anil B. Divan, who was an eminent jurist and a senior advocate in the Supreme Court.

The center has a remarkable collection of over 1500 books, including authoritative volumes such as the All India Reporter (AIR) manuals , law commission reports and jurisprudence from the United Kingdom (UK) and the United States (US). It also features insightful commentaries on national and international laws. This generous contribution by the Anil Diwan Foundation ensures that Anil Diwan's extensive knowledge and expertise in the legal field continue to benefit and inspire future generations of legal professionals.

Project MPSIC (Madhya Pradesh State Information Commission)

Project Madhya Pradesh State Information Commission is an initiative borne out from an MoU between Office of Rahul Singh, Information Commissioner MPSIC and Zenith to design strategies for improving the system of transparency and accountability in Madhya Pradesh. Shri Rahul Singh is an Information Commissioner in the Madhya Pradesh State Information Commission, a statutory body constituted under the Right to Information Act, 2005 (RTI Act, 2005).

Through this collaboration, Zenith provides research assistance in hearing and deciding the appeals and complaints; facilitates the Internship and; manages a RTI helpline, to assist citizens file RTI applications, appeals and create better understanding of the RTI Act. Zenith also facilitates awareness camps, training programs, workshops and seminars, to advance the understanding of the Act and its importance for transparency and accountability.
