EMPOWERING COMMUNITIES

TO ACCESS JUSTICE

We build capabilities in community members through an ecosystem of resources, skills and tools of law to identify and voice their struggles, and act towards designing innovative justice solutions

WHY COMMUNITY EMPOWERMENT?

We are based in the Chambal region, where the issues of social justice are borne from the unequal structures (class, caste and gender) leading to violent crimes and extreme discrimination. Justice is inaccessible to the marginalised communities, particularly the Adivasi and Dalit communities in this region, due to:

  • ◆ Lack of understanding of rights
  • ◆ Inability to exercise and enforce their right
  • ◆ Failure of the state to deliver justice
  • ◆ This leads to these communities remaining
  • marginalised both socially and economically
