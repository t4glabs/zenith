Prasant

Lawyer, Samvidhi Sashakti kendra, Gwalior

Prashant is a young lawyer practicing in the District Court of Gwalior. He hails from a nearby district of Mainpuri in Uttar Pradesh. A graduate from Madhav Law College, Gwalior, he handles criminal litigation in Gwalior. Involved in student politics during his college days, he is well connected in the lawyers fraternity and is a guide to many young students venturing into the legal field. He is a wanderer and loves exploring food places.
