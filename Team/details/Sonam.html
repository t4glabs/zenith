Sonam

Center Head

Sonam is the centre head at Samudhayik Adhikar Kendra. A natural leader, he worked with many non-profit organizations and over time developed an interest in supporting people to fight injustices. His experience engaging with the community and various government departments has made him an expert in navigating through the justice delivery institutions especially related to government entitlements and schemes. Having faced family difficulties, he started working at a very young age and could not complete his formal education. He recently enrolled into a graduation program. Sonam is an athlete and loves to play cricket.
