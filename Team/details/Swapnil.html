Swapnil

Co-founder

Swapnil is a lawyer and a SRUTI fellow. He graduated from National Law University, Odisha and after working in a law firm in Delhi for a year, quit to move to Shivpuri where he set up the first legal aid clinic at Zenith. During his law school days he worked with the Center for Policy Research & Namati in Gujarat and Karnataka where he got exposed to the existing ground realities and gaps in law.. He believes that there is a need to build an army of justice makers at the grassroots. Passionate and committed, his energetic and humorous presence puts everyone at ease. He is currently experimenting with storytelling as a tool for change. He is a footballer and you will often see him with his camera taking photos of people and places.
