Meenakshi

Lawyer, Samvidhi Sashakti kendra, Gwalior

Meenakshi is a young lawyer who handles cases of gender violence in Gwalior. She completed her LLM from Gwalior in 2021 and quite recently became a registered member of the bar. She believes that it is difficult for girls to find safe spaces to work as a lawyer in tier 2 and tier 3 cities/towns and wants to change that. She supports her family immensely and finds ‘dancing’ meditative.
