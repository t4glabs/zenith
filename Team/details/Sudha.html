Sudha

Community Worker

Sudha is a community worker at Samudayik Adhikar Kendra. Committed and value driven, she found her calling doing social work. Sudha joined us during the challenging times of the COVID-19 pandemic, and persisted to provide medical support to hundreds - having a medical kit and diary by her side. Working for the people brings her immense joy and fulfillment. Having suffered caste discrimination all her life, she works with dalit women - understanding their needs, and providing support to them. Despite initial lack of support from her family, she persevered, and now, supports her family financially. Sudha is not just a loving mother of two but also the most compassionate person, striving to make a positive impact in the lives of the most vulnerable.
