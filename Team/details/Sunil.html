Sunil Kumar

Community Manager, Samvidhi Sashakti kendra, Gwalior

Sunil leads the community work at Gwalior. Hailing from a marginalised community, he underwent great difficulties to educate himself. A post graduate in economics, he recently completed his law degree to support communities to access justice in Gwalior. He has an experience of over 10 years in the social sector and has worked with various organisations on skillbuilding, livelihood and elderly rights. He likes to listen to old hindi songs and loves spending time with his wife and a four year old daughter.
