Sunil Kumar

Community Manager

Sunil leads the community work at Gwalior. Hailing from a marginalized community, he underwent great difficulties to educate himself. A post graduate in economics, he recently completed his law degree to support communities to access justice in Gwalior. He has an experience of over 10 years in the social sector and has worked with various organizations on skillbuilding, livelihood and elderly rights. He likes to listen to old hindi songs and loves spending time with his wife and a four year old daughter.
