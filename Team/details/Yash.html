Yash

Content Creator

Yash is a graduate from Hansraj College, Delhi University. Born and brought up in Gwalior, his journey with Zenith began as an intern, during which he gained invaluable insights about the local environmental issues. Throughout his college life, he has interacted with a lot of workers, professors and activists working at the grassroots. His experience at Sambhaavnaa Institute, Palampur and rural areas of Shivpuri enriched his understanding of the society, fostering a deeper empathy for the challenges faced by different communities. Motivated with this awareness, he is determined to dedicate his efforts and skills towards empowerment of the people. He is a skateboarder, who loves visual storytelling and enjoys being close to nature.
