Bhawna

Lead, Design & Communication

Bhawna is a designer and a post-graduate from Ambedkar University. A social designer, researcher and an artist with varied experience across disciplines, she's trying to find meaning at the intersections of co-design, research and social justice. She helps Zenith navigate their design language. She co-founded a social design collaborative called Barabar which attempts to focus on designing for justice by keeping communities at the center. She is also an illustrator and loves to comment on contemporary politics through pop vibrant colours through her illustrations. She’s the happiest when she’s interacting with people and asking them questions to understand their context and lives.
