EMPOWERING COMMUNITIES

TO ACCESS JUSTICE

We build capabilities in community members through an ecosystem of resources, skills and tools of law to identify and voice their struggles, and act towards designing innovative justice solutions

WHY COMMUNITY EMPOWERMENT?

We are based in the Chambal Gwalior region, where the issues of social justice are borne from the unequal structures (class, caste and gender) leading to violent crimes and extreme discrimination. Justice is inaccessible to the marginalised communities, particularly the Adivasi and Dalit communities in this region, due to:

Lack of understanding of rights

Inability to exercise and enforce their right

Failure of the state to deliver justice

What’s our approach?

LEGAL AID

Providing legal support and equipping people with knowledge and tools to understand and enforce their rights.

BUILDING JUSTICE MAKING CAPABILITIES

Empowering people with justice making capabilities - activating their agency to solve justice problems.

GRASSROOT STORYTELLING

Raising awareness and inspiring action through stories from the grassroots.

We believe in power of the people to become justice enablers

There are no linear solutions to justice. The heart of our vision lies in democratizing power to enable action towards finding hyperlocal justice solutions.
